About Dala🚲na

💡 Our Mission

Dala🚲na is a hyperlocal delivery service born in the heart of the city. We aim to make daily essentials—from panaderia goods to pharmacy items—accessible and delivered with care within minutes.

📍 Community-Centered

We prioritize local shops and community riders, helping small businesses grow while making life easier for customers.

🚴 Subong Dayon!

We don’t just deliver quickly—we deliver with intention, love, and a personal touch. Every order supports a chain of local heroes.

🌱 Why We Started

Dala🚲na began during the pandemic as a passion project to support neighbors, sari-sari stores, and riders who needed a way to earn.
