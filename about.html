About Dala🚲na

🌟 Our Mission / Aming Misyon

English: Dala🚲na is built to deliver daily essentials quickly and lovingly—right to your doorstep. Whether you need groceries, medicine, bread, or lunch, we bring them to you safely and affordably.

Hiligaynon: Ang Dala🚲na gintukod agud maghatag sang kinahanglanon sa adlaw-adlaw sa imo balay—may kabaskog kag paghigugma. Kung kinahanglan mo pagkaon, bulong, pan, o tanghalian, kami ang magadala.

🏘️ Why We Exist / Ngaman Ari Kami

English: We saw a need for faster, more caring local delivery in neighborhoods like yours. Dala🚲na empowers small merchants and riders while making your life easier.

Hiligaynon: Nakita namon ang panginahanglan sang mas madasig kag maalwan nga delivery. Ang Dala🚲na nagasuporta sa local nga negosyo kag mga riders, samtang ginapadali ang imo pangabuhi.

🤝 Community First / Komunidad Una

English: We partner with nearby bakeries, pharmacies, markets, and food stalls. Every order supports your local community.

Hiligaynon: Nakipagpartner kami sa mga local nga panaderya, botika, merkado, kag karinderya. Kada order mo, bulig ini sa aton komunidad.

📞 Get in Touch / Kontaka Kami

English: Have a suggestion? Want to become a rider or merchant? Contact us anytime via WhatsApp, Facebook, or Email.

Hiligaynon: May sugyot ka? Gusto magrider ukon magpartner nga merchant? Kontaka kami bisan san-o.
